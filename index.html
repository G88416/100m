Lotto Reverse Engineering

Brute Force Strategy

The simplest approach that tests every possible seed within the specified range to find matches with the observed draws.

Pros: Guaranteed to find the seed if it's within the search range.

Cons: Computationally expensive for large search ranges.

Statistical Analysis Strategy

Analyzes the statistical properties of the observed draws to narrow down the search space for potential seeds.

Pros: Faster than brute force for certain patterns.

Cons: May miss seeds that don't match the statistical profile.

Pattern Recognition

Pattern Recognition Strategy

Identifies repeating patterns in the observed draws to predict the PRNG state without exhaustive search.

Pros: Can be very fast for PRNGs with identifiable patterns.

Cons: Requires more observed draws and works best with weaker PRNGs.

Hybrid Approach

Combines statistical analysis with pattern recognition to optimize the search process.

Pros: Balances speed and accuracy.

Cons: More complex implementation.
